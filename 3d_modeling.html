Point of Sale Display

Motogyro is a motorsports camera brand that I created for class. The gyroscopic design stabilizes the camera while the rider has it on their helmet. For the trademark, I create a silhouette of two motorcycle helmets next to eachother. The shape of the design is also a play on the gyroscopic design of the product. I incorporated the trademark into the display by making it the side supports.
